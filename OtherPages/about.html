Our Journey

The Vision behind MyGameList.

We want to achieve an easy game tracking for gamers, an easy game progress check and a possibility to compare with your friends.

Our Mission

We saw other websites like MyAnimeList and got inspired by it to do our own list to track the games we play with our own scores and seeing our friends opinions on games they play.

Data Driven

We are based on real and legit numbers and facts without any paid reviews.

Community First

The opinion of our users are very important to us. Thats why your scorings on the games are more weighted than the ratings from global critic aggregates.

Open Future

We are constantly improving our website and listen to our user's feedbacks.

Project Milestones

  • 2026:This year was the start of MyGameList where we thought about our ideas for this site, got team members of all kinds to help us with the site and do our best to finish everything flawlessly before release.
  • Future:In the future, we plan to keep working on this project by adding many features to keep this fresh for our users.

How it's built

MyGameList is built with modern web technologies to ensure speed and reliability. Currently using HTML5, CSS3 (Modern Grid/Flexbox) and Vanilla JavaScript for maximum performance.
