How do I add a game to my list?

Just click on the "Add to List" button on the game you would like to add. It automatically saves it on your profile.

Why cant I login to my account?

Sometimes it's an server issue, but if the login button is bugged, then try to reload the page.

Why cant I find the game I want to add?

It might be because it is too new or it didn't get added to our database yet, just try again later.
