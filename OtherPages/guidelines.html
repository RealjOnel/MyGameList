Community Guidelines – MyGameList

Welcome to MyGameList! We are committed to fostering a positive and inclusive gaming community. To ensure that everyone has a safe and enjoyable experience, we have established the following guidelines for all users. By using our platform, you agree to adhere to these rules and help us maintain a respectful environment for gamers of all backgrounds.

Be Respectful

  1. Treat all users with respect — no insults, bullying, or harassment.
  2. Discrimination based on race, gender, sexuality, religion, or origin will not be tolerated.
  3. Friendly debates are okay — personal attacks are not.

Keep It Clean

  1. No hate speech, NSFW, or illegal content.
  2. Avoid posting spam, advertisements, or self-promotion without permission.
  3. Use appropriate language in forums and messages.

Be Honest in Reviews

  1. Share your real opinions and experiences with games.
  2. Don’t post fake, copied, or manipulated reviews.
  3. Respect others’ tastes — gaming is for everyone.

Forum Etiquette

  1. Stay on topic — post in the correct category.
  2. Don’t flood threads or post the same message repeatedly.
  3. Help new users feel welcome and guide them when needed.

Friendships & Messages

  1. Don’t share personal information (like address, phone number, or passwords).
  2. Private messages must follow the same rules as public posts.
  3. If someone blocks you, respect their choice.

Protect Your Privacy

  1. Don’t share personal information (like address, phone number, or passwords).
  2. Report suspicious users or activity to the moderators.

Moderation & Enforcement

  1. Our team may remove content or restrict accounts that break these guidelines.
  2. Repeated or serious violations can lead to temporary or permanent bans.
  3. If you feel a moderation decision was unfair, contact support politely.

Have Fun & Contribute!

  1. Share your passion for gaming.
  2. Write helpful reviews, join discussions, and make new friends.
  3. Help us keep this community positive and creative.
