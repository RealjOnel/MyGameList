How we calculate the scores

1. User Scoring (The Pulse)

Every rating on MyGameList is weighted by user activity. To prevent "review bombing," our algorithm prioritizes users with a verified backlog and consistent rating history.

2. Metacritic Integration

We pull real-time data from global critic aggregates. This provides a baseline of quality from a technical and narrative perspective, as seen by professionals.

3. The Comparison Logic

The magic happens when we compare both. We highlight "Hidden Gems" (high User, low Meta) and "Overhyped Titles" (high Meta, low User).
